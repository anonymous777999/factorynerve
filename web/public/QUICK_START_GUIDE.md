### Step 1: Log In

> **[SCREENSHOT: Login page — email field, password field, SIGN IN button]**

1. Open Chrome or any browser on your phone/computer
2. Go to `https://[yourname].factorynerve.com`
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **SIGN IN**

---

### Step 2: Your Dashboard

After login, you'll see the **Steel Batches** page — this is your main dashboard.

> **[SCREENSHOT: Steel Batches page showing batch list with columns: Batch ID, Item Code, Production Date, Input Qty, Output Qty, Loss%, Severity]**

**What you see here:**
- Every production batch recorded in your factory
- **Green** severity = normal (loss < 3%)
- **Yellow** severity = watch (loss 3-5%)
- **Red** severity = critical (loss > 5%) — investigate immediately
- **Theft indicator:** If a batch shows 10%+ loss with no explanation, someone may be stealing material

**Try this now:**
- Tap any batch row to see full details
- Notice the heat number for traceability
- Check the input vs output quantities

---

### Step 3: Check Attendance

> **[SCREENSHOT: Attendance page showing punch in/out or live view]**

Click **Attendance** in the sidebar (or go to `/attendance`).

**See:**
- Who punched in today
- Who's missing
- Live count of people on the floor

**If someone forgot to punch:**
- They can request regularization
- You (or supervisor) approve it

---

### Step 4: Check Inventory

> **[SCREENSHOT: Inventory stock levels page showing item list with quantities]**

Click **Inventory** in the sidebar (or go to `/steel/inventory`).

**See:**
- All raw materials and finished goods
- Current stock in kg
- Reorder points (low stock warning)

---

### Step 5: Review & Approve

> **[SCREENSHOT: Approvals page showing pending items]**

Click **Work Queue** or **Approvals** in the sidebar.

**What needs your approval:**
- DPR entries submitted by operators
- Attendance regularization requests
- Dispatch requests (for large dispatches)

**To approve:** Click the item, review, click **Approve** or **Reject** with a reason.

---

### Step 6: View Your Daily PDF Report

> **[SCREENSHOT: Owner Daily PDF — one-page summary with revenue, production, leakage]**

Go to `/steel/owner-daily-pdf` (or find it in Steel section).

This is a **one-page PDF** showing:
- Today's revenue
- Production vs target
- Top losses/theft alerts
- Pending approvals

Download and save it. Forward to your partner or CA.

---

### Step 7: Ask AI Questions

> **[SCREENSHOT: AI page with NLQ input and response]**

Click **AI** in the sidebar.

**Type questions in plain English like:**
- "How much did we produce this week?"
- "Show me batches with high loss"
- "Which customer hasn't paid yet?"

The AI will answer from your factory data.
