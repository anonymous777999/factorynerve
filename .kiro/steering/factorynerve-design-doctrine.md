# FactoryNerve Operational Design Doctrine
**Version 1.0 — Internal Design Law**

This doctrine governs every design and implementation decision in the FactoryNerve OS frontend.
When any rule in this document conflicts with "looking cool" or "being dramatic," the rule wins.
The operator's mental temperature must go DOWN when they use this system, never up.

---

## GOVERNING PRINCIPLE

The system must feel: CALM · PREMIUM · TRUSTWORTHY · OPERATIONALLY INTELLIGENT
The system must NOT feel: tactical · sci-fi · cyberpunk · hacker · dashboard-like

---
