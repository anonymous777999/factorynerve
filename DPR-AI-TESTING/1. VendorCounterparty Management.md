1.  SteelPurchaseOrder  +  SteelPurchaseOrderLine  (PO header + lines)
2.  SteelGoodsReceipt  +  SteelGoodsReceiptLine  (GRN header + lines)
3.  SteelPurchaseInvoice  (supplier bill — simpler than sales invoice, no dispatch)
4. Minor: add  party_type  +  bank_details  to  SteelCustomer 
And 7 new models already existing and fully reusable:
-  SteelCustomer  → vendor (2 new fields needed)
-  SteelInventoryTransaction  → auto GRN-to-stock (fully ready)
-  SteelCustomerPayment  → vendor payment (fully ready)
-  SteelCustomerPaymentAllocation  → payment allocation (fully ready)
-  SteelInventoryItem  → purchase item catalog (fully ready)
-  AuditLog  → audit trail (fully ready)
-  SteelSalesInvoice  → template pattern for purchase invoice
